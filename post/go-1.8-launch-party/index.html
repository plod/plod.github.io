Cardiff Go 1.8 launch party

Posted by Daniel T. Morgan on Thursday Mar 2 2017
In Development, Golang
Tags go, golang, templates, themes, development

On Febuary the 16th go released it’s newest version 1.8. You can read about this release here. I presented at the Cardiff Go meetup group’s release party on the 15th. Considering how I am not the greatest speaker in public, it went very well. I was pretty nervous to start, but nerves slowly faded especially when I got to talk about my favourite new features (http2 pushPromice etc). You can see my slides here.

Great talk from @plodtv at our #GoReleaseParty and congrats to @rossmcf on winning our raffle - special thanks to @github for the prize!

— Cardiff Go Meetup (@cdfgolang) February 15, 2017

Everyone seemed to enjoy and I got a lot of positive feedback afterwards.
